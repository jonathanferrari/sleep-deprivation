Additional Resources

  • Hopkins Medicine - The Effects of Sleep Deprivation
    • Highlights risks such as type 2 diabetes, heart disease, obesity, and impaired cognitive abilities.
  • Healthline - The Effects of Sleep Deprivation on Your Body
    • Explains impacts on the central nervous, immune, and cardiovascular systems, including mood swings and heart disease.
  • Cleveland Clinic - Sleep Deprivation
    • Defines stages of deprivation and links it to increased blood pressure, diabetes, and memory issues.
  • National Heart, Lung and Blood Institute - How Sleep Affects Your Health
    • Details effects on hormonal balance, heart health, and cognitive functions.
  • AASM Sleep Education - College Students Aren't Getting Enough Sleep
    • Focuses on academic impacts, health risks, and lifestyle factors affecting sleep.
  • Shelley D Hershner, Ronald D Chervin - Causes and Consequences of Sleepiness Among College Students
    • Examines the prevalence and interventions for improving sleep among students.
  • Carlyle Smith, Danielle Smith - Sleep Patterns and Academic Impact
    • Discusses "social jet lag," memory impairment, and risky behavior linked to poor sleep.
